Reflow vs Repaint vs Composite (Browser Rendering)

1. Reflow (Layout)
   - What: Recalculates element size/position/layout.
   - Triggers: width, height, margin, padding, display, DOM add/remove, font-size.
   - Cost: Expensive (can affect whole page).
   - Example: element.style.width = "200px";

2. Repaint
   - What: Redraws pixels without changing layout.
   - Triggers: color, background-color, visibility, text-decoration.
   - Cost: Medium (only redraws visuals).
   - Example: element.style.backgroundColor = "red";

3. Composite
   - What: Moves or combines existing painted layers.
   - Triggers: transform (translate, scale, rotate), opacity, will-change.
   - Cost: Very cheap (GPU optimized).
   - Example: element.style.transform = "translateX(100px)";

Performance Hierarchy:
Reflow > Repaint > Composite
