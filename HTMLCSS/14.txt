ðŸ“Œ WHAT IS `cubic-bezier()`?

- `cubic-bezier()` is a CSS timing function used in transitions and animations to control the **speed curve**.
- It gives you **fine-tuned control** over how an animation progresses over time.
- Instead of relying on pre-defined timing functions (`ease`, `linear`, etc.), you define your own custom
 easing.
